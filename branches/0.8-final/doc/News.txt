Wormux, the free Worms-like is available in 0.8.

For everyone,
Its a funny mass murder game for 2 to 4 players (local and remote).

using sensible weapons (like bazooka, TNT, football balls !)
The aime is be the only one team !

In the news of this release :

    - Index Server (with all games)
    - 30 languages !
    - New designs
    - Joystick ehanced
